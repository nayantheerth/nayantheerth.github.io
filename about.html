About Myself

I am Nayantheerth Akhil, a Computer Science student with a strong interest in low-level systems, embedded electronics, and robotics. I enjoy understanding how technology works beneath the surface, from operating system internals to hardware-level communication between devices. Much of my work involves building and experimenting with systems such as drones, microcontroller-based projects, and custom communication protocols. Through these projects I focus on practical problem solving, system design, and learning by building real hardware and software together.
